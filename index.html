GLOBAL TERRORISM

Welcome to our data visualization project on global terrorism. Through this project, we aim to explore and understand the trends and patterns of terrorist attacks across different categories. Our project includes graphs on cases by weapon, cases by country, cases by talibans, and cases by isi. We have used the Global Terrorism Database as our primary data source. Our interactive visualizations will help you gain insights into the various factors and groups involved in global terrorism. Join us on this journey to understand the complex nature of this issue.
